About Me

I'm Alix Webb, and I'm currently a Web Developer in-training. I was raised in many places throughout the Eastern U.S., but I currently live in Greenville, SC. In 2014, I graduated from Virginia Tech with a Bachelor of Science in Industrial and Systems Engineering. Post-graduation, I used my engineering skills to work for an automotive supplier for six years. Because I wasn't feeling fulfilled, I sought out a new career direction in hopes of finding my dream job. I hope to become a Full Stack Web Developer, but more than that, I hope to work for a company that has a great purpose. My dream company does something for the greater good. It is a company that is full of people who work hard but also know how to have fun. It is a company that deeply cares for the well-being of its associates and pushes them to continuously learn and grow.
